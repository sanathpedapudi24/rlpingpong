03 — Core Insight

Why Symbolic Regression?

Neural networks solve problems — but can we understand how?

01
The Black Box Problem
Neural networks achieve superhuman performance — but their decisions are trapped in millions of opaque parameters. What did it actually learn?
02
The Distillation Idea
Use the neural network as a teacher. Generate predictions across thousands of states, then let symbolic regression discover the formula that replicates its decisions — a mathematical compression of learned behavior.
03
The Payoff
The result is interpretable, lightning-fast (no matrix math at inference), and provably compact. The entire policy collapses to:
sin(x₅ + sin(x₃ + x₅ × (−1.692))) + 0.976
